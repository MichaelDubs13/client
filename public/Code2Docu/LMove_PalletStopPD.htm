Functional description

LMove_PalletStopPD serves as the primary controller for the pallet stops used on conveyors.

A pallet stop is used to stop pallets or buffer parts on the conveyor. Most Pallet stops have a dampening feature to reduce the impact on the pallet and the part. The pallet stops are designed in a way to return to their raised state once air is de-energized to them. Pallet stops can be configured with or without feedback.

This block does not support individual actutation of the dampners/cushions on the stoppers.
